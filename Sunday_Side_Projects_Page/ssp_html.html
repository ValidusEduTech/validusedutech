Sunday Side Projects

SUNDAY SIDE PROJECT schema is designed to encourage students to work on practical, hands-on projects outside of their regular academic schedule, typically on Sundays. These projects allow students to apply the mathematics, physics, and programming concepts they've learned in a creative and exploratory way.

The schema emphasizes independent or group work where students take the lead in developing their own projects, experimenting with tools like Geogebra, p5.js, and Javascript. By showcasing their programming skills and collaborating with peers, students gain a deeper understanding of concepts while building real-world applications. This approach fosters innovation, critical thinking, and problem-solving beyond the classroom, helping students bridge the gap between theory and practice.
